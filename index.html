This is Me

Network Engineer

A network engineer is a technology professional who has the necessary skills to plan, implement and oversee the computer networks that support in-house voice, data, video and wireless network services.

Network engineer salaries range from $46,500 to more than $115,000 annually, depending upon skills and experience. Engineers can also earn bonuses, and some employers offer profit-sharing in addition. Network engineers work 40 hours a week, but they may be called in for weekends.
